Facts about shakespeare himself!

1. William Shakespeare was an English playwright, poet, and actor born in 1564 in Stratford-upon-Avon, who died in 1616 which was his 52nd Birthday.

2. Shakespeare's Darkest play was "Titus Andronicus" about Titus, a general in the Roman army, presents Tamora, Queen of the Goths, as a slave to the new Roman emperor, Saturninus. Saturninus takes her as his wife.

3. Shakespeare made up words that we stil use to this day, e.g "Swagger" (was made more known due to him and was not made by him.), "In a pickle", "Bedroom", and "Good Riddance" many more words are made by him this is only the tip of the ice berg.
